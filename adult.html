Brands to Know

松竹梅 (shouchikubai)
"Pine, Bamboo, Plum"
A widely available, everyday sake brand family. The three characters are also a classic Japanese ranking set (matsu = top, take = middle, ume = third), often seen on menus for grading dishes.
鏡月 (kyougetsu)
"Mirror Moon"
A clear, mild spirit popular for mixed drinks like green tea or lemon highballs. Easy to drink and common at izakayas.
日高見 (hitakami)
A junmai sake brand from Miyagi Prefecture
The name comes from an old poetic term for the Tohoku region. Known for a clean, rice-forward taste.
