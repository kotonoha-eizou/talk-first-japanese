Brands to Know

エロ (ero)
"Erotic"
A common Japanese word referring to erotic or sexually explicit content.
AV (ēbui)
"Adult Video"
The standard Japanese abbreviation for adult videos and adult entertainment media.
無修正 (mushūsei)
"Uncensored"
Refers to content without mosaic censorship, often associated with overseas-produced adult media.
素人 (shirōto)
"Amateur"
A term used for people presented as non-professional performers.
人妻 (hitodzuma)
"Married Woman"
A genre term referring to married women.
巨乳 (kyonyū)
"Large Breasts"
A Japanese term describing large breasts.
爆乳 (bakunyū)
"Huge Breasts"
A stronger expression than kyonyū, describing extremely large breasts.
貧乳 (hinnyū)
"Small Breasts"
A term describing small breasts, often used in adult or fictional contexts.
熟女 (jukujo)
"Mature Woman"
A genre term referring to mature adult women.
ギャル (gyaru)
"Gyaru / Trendy Girl"
A Japanese fashion and subculture term often used as a genre category.
制服 (seifuku)
"School Uniform"
Refers to Japanese-style uniforms, commonly associated with school-themed content.
コスプレ (kosupure)
"Cosplay"
A Japanese loanword meaning costume play or dressing as a character.
痴漢 (chikan)
